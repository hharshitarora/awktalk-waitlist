How AwkTalk Works

Simple steps to more confident, less awkward conversations.

1

Start Your Conversation

Open the AwkTalk app and begin your conversation as you normally would. Our AI will listen in the background (with your permission) and start analyzing the discussion for awkward moments.

Perfect for first dates, networking events, family gatherings, or any situation where conversations might get uncomfortable.

Receive Real-time Alerts

As the conversation progresses, AwkTalk provides discreet notifications about:

  • Awkward silences and pauses
  • Potentially uncomfortable topics
  • Tone mismatches between speakers
  • Opportunities to improve the flow

These alerts appear subtly on your device without interrupting the conversation.
